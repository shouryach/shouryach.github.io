What We Do

Adelaide test and tagging is an electrical and fire safety testing and tagging company With professional, fully qualified technicians and customer satisfaction being our top focus, we are happy to answer any questions and provide you with the right advice for your company’s requirements.

We believe that safety is paramount within any industry, and endeavour to assist you in ensuring your employees are safe at work. We strive to achieve this through continuous improvement and establishing exceptional customer relationships.

Adelaide test and tagging assists in ensuring workplace compliance to specific electrical and fire safety requirements, not only by providing relevant compliance information to our clients, but also by providing a highly focused service that exceeds recommended regimes.
